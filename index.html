Our story

Spaces that feel alive.

Lush Hospitality began in 2020 to craft places inspired by nature — open spaces, green corners, and calming vibes. A collection of luxury cafés, lounges, boutique hotels, cinemas, and catering where guests come not just to eat, but to feel.

Since 2020 Growing into a name trusted for comfort, creativity, and soulful experiences.
